Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs eggs
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

Directions

  1. Pre-heat Oven to 375 °F
  2. In a large mixing bowlream butter, peanut butter, and sugars
  3. beat in eggs.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  5. stir into butter mixture.
  6. Refrigerate dough for 1 hour
  7. Roll dough into 1 inch balls and put on baking sheets.
  8. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a crisscross pattern.
  9. Bake in a preheated 375 degrees F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown.
